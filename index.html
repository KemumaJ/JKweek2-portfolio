AKAN Name

Naming of children is considered a very special ceremony in most parts of the world. For instance, in Ghana, Ivory coast and Togo, a child is
according to the day of the week they are born. Each day has a corresponding Akan name which makes it interesting to know yours.
The Akan people from Ghana are given their first name as 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they
were born. Curious to know your Akan name?....Enter the day, month and year you were born and enter!
