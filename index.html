AKAN Names

Naming of children is considered a very special ceremony in most parts of the world. For instance, in Ghana, Ivory coast and Togo, a child is named according to the day of the week they are born.
The Akan people from Ghana are given their first name as 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they
